The true speed of a car

Startpage

The speed of %[ in.person.name ]%'s car is %[out.total.speed]% km/h. And what is your car's?

Time

What do costs have to do with the speed? Within a year, how much time does %[ in.person.name ]% spend to travel by %[ in.transport.vehicle ]%?

We have to take into consideration not only the %[out.time.way]% hours %[ in.person.salutatory ]% spends on driving, but also the time %[ in.person.salutatory ]% worked to afford the %[ in.transport.vehicle ]%: %[out.time.expenses]%.

In total, it is therefore %[out.total.time]% hours.
